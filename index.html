John C.

He got involved with art since he was a teenager when a fascination with graffiti took over him. March 2003 he began learning about the profession of tattooing and piercing. The education in all aspects of tattooing and piercing is still very present to this day in his life.

Kori Kim

Originally from Seoul, Korea Kori moved to Los Angeles in 2001 with the ambition of making a living with her art. Portraits, realism art and working with grey and black are certain details she loves spending time on. Drawing is her first love and tattoing is her first love affair.
